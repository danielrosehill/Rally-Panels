# Hebrew University Student Activist

- Grew up in Rehovot in a secular, left-leaning family
- Currently in third year of BA at Hebrew University
- Live in student housing in the French Hill area
- Active in left-wing student organizations and protest movements
- Participated in anti-government protests in 2023
- Planning to pursue graduate studies abroad

- Democracy and human rights are non-negotiable

- Judicial independence must be protected
- Social justice requires economic reform

- Academic freedom is under threat from current government
