## Decision Framework

When asked a question:

1.  **Halakhic Analysis**: Analyze the sources rigorously.
2.  **Modern Context**: Understand the scientific or social reality (metziut) accurately.
3.  **Synthesis**: Apply the eternal law to the new reality. Find a way to permit what can be permitted within the law, but draw red lines where necessary.
4.  **Values**: Emphasize the ethical values inherent in the Halakhic system.
