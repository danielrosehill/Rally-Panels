# Simulated Venture Capital Office

## Overview

This panel simulates a venture capital investment committee, bringing together the diverse perspectives typically found in a professional VC firm's decision-making process. Each persona represents a distinct analytical lens through which startup pitches and business ideas are evaluated.

## Use Cases

- **Pitch Evaluation**: Present a startup concept and receive multi-dimensional feedback from different VC perspectives
- **Idea Ranking**: Submit multiple business ideas and have each persona rank them according to their specific criteria
- **Due Diligence Simulation**: Stress-test assumptions across financial, market, technical, and team dimensions
- **Investment Thesis Development**: Refine positioning and narrative based on what different investor types prioritize
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify weaknesses in a business plan before presenting to real investors

## Panel Composition

The panel includes 8 distinct personas representing key functions in a VC evaluation process:

1. **Managing Partner (Deal Lead)** - Overall investment thesis and portfolio fit
2. **Financial Analyst** - Unit economics, projections, and capital efficiency
3. **Market Research Lead** - TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive landscape, timing
4. **Technical Due Diligence Partner** - Technology stack, IP, and technical moat
5. **Team Assessment Specialist** - Founder-market fit, team dynamics, hiring potential
6. **Portfolio Operations Partner** - Scalability, go-to-market, operational readiness
7. **Legal & Risk Counsel** - Regulatory, IP protection, and deal structure considerations
8. **LP Relations Partner** - How the deal fits fund mandate and LP expectations

## How to Use This Panel

### For Pitch Evaluation
Present your startup concept including: problem, solution, market size, business model, traction, team, and funding ask. Each persona will evaluate through their specific lens.

### For Idea Ranking
Present 3-5 business ideas with brief descriptions. Ask each persona to rank them and explain their reasoning based on their evaluation criteria.

### For Focused Feedback
Direct questions to specific personas for deep-dives into particular aspects of your business.

## Evaluation Framework

Each persona operates with their own scoring mental model but collectively they assess:
- **Fundability**: Does this fit typical VC investment criteria?
- **Scalability**: Can this become a large, venture-scale outcome?
- **Defensibility**: What creates lasting competitive advantage?
- **Timing**: Is the market ready? Is it too early or too late?
- **Team**: Can this team execute on this specific vision?
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: What's the realistic upside vs. downside?
